## What I learned (the stuff you don't see on the homepage)

- Alignment matters more than code on cross-department platforms
- Content modeling is product design
- Performance is a feature, especially on maps
- "Easy" user experiences are the result of many small decisions

---

## Tech stack (the pieces that made it ship)

- Next.js
- Sanity CMS
- MapLibre
- Sentry
- SimpleBooking and WAG integrations
- Custom admin tools and email flows
- Vercel Analytics and Google Analytics
